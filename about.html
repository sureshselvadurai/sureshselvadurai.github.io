About Me

01 / Background

Hi — I'm Suresh Raja Selvadurai. I'm currently a Machine Learning Engineer at SelectDr in San Francisco, where I build production healthcare recommendation systems and applied LLM products. Before that, I was an Automation Software Intern at Guardant Health, a Consultant in Data Science and AI at KPMG (client: Goldman Sachs), a Data Scientist at Kissht, and a Domain Name Analyst at NameKart — 7+ years across ML, backend, and automation.

I hold an M.S. in Computer Science from the University of San Francisco (GPA 3.95/4.00) and a B.Tech. in Civil Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology Patna.

02 / What I Do

I work across the full ML lifecycle — from ranking and retrieval to shipping the product in front of real users. At SelectDr, I built recommendation systems that rank healthcare providers on clinical fit, quality, experience, distance, insurance, and patient signals, and shipped a multi-agent LLM chatbot with structured conversation state, retrieval, validation, and safety routing for guided healthcare search.

  • Backend & data: FastAPI APIs serving 1,000+ requests/second, and Databricks/PySpark, PostgreSQL/PostGIS, and OpenSearch pipelines over 100M+ healthcare records.
  • ML: PyTorch, TensorFlow, scikit-learn, XGBoost, LLMs, RAG, recommender systems, NLP, model evaluation, and A/B testing.
  • Frontend & infra: React and Next.js workflows, AWS, Docker, Kubernetes, Terraform, and CI/CD.

03 / Elsewhere

Outside of my day job, I'm interested in full-stack development, fintech, and automation — I've built a personal-finance Android app, credit-risk models, and a handful of side projects (see Projects). I hold Meta Front-End Developer and IBM Machine Learning certifications, and published research on the compressibility behavior of bentonites during my undergrad.
